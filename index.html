About Us

President, Lia D'Agostino, has trained all across the world, so you are assured of emerging from our showroom with the most gorgeous gown and matching accessories. Her life's work is bridal couture. Along with her professional staff, Lia promises that the look you've always dreamed of will indeed become a beautiful reality.
